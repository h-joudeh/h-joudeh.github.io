Hamdi Joudeh: Research

My research focuses on fundamental and algorithmic aspects of wireless communications. I am particularly interested in problems related to (robust) interference management in emerging and conventional wireless communication networks.

Topics of current and previous interest include:

  • Optimality of treating interference as noise (TIN)

  • Degrees-of-freedom (DoF) and generalized degrees-of-freedom (GDoF)

  • Cache-aided and content-aware wireless networks

  • Robust beamforming and precoding

A complete list of my papers is available through my Publications page.
