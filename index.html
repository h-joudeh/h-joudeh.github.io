I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e). My research interests lie broadly in information theory, communications, and signal processing. My current work focuses on wireless communication and sensing, source and channel coding, and exponential error bounds. I also teach undergraduate courses in electrical engineering on information theory and probability.

Research Interests

  • Information Theory

  • Communications

  • Signal Processing

Selected Recent Works

  • Exponential error bounds for information bottleneck source coding problems

  • Joint communication and parameter estimation in MIMO channels

  • On Gaussian codes and mismatched decoding for fading MIMO multiple-access channels

  • Dual domain expurgated error exponents for source coding with side information

  • Error exponents for oblivious relaying and connections to source coding with a helper

  • Ensemble-tight second-order asymptotics and exponents for guessing-based decoding with abandonment
