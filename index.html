I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e). I am a member of the Information and Communication Theory (ICT) Lab within the Signal Processing Systems (SPS) Group. My research interests lie broadly in information theory, communications, and signal processing. My current work focuses on wireless communication and sensing, source and channel coding, and exponential error bounds. I also teach undergraduate courses in electrical engineering on information theory and probability.

Research Interests

  • Information Theory

  • Communications

  • Signal Processing

Selected Recent Works

  • Soft guessing under logarithmic loss allowing errors and variable-length source coding

  • Exponential error bounds for information bottleneck source coding problems

  • Joint communication and parameter estimation in MIMO channels

  • Dual domain expurgated error exponents for source coding with side information

  • Error exponents for oblivious relaying and connections to source coding with a helper

  • Strong converse exponent for remote lossy source coding

  • Ensemble-tight second-order asymptotics and exponents for guessing-based decoding with abandonment
