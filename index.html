I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e). I am part of the Information and Communication Theory (ICT) Lab, within the Signal Processing Systems (SPS) Group. My research interests are broadly in information theory, wireless communications, and sensing. Topics of current focus include: joint communication and sensing, source and channel coding, and error exponents. I also teach courses on information theory and probability to bachelor students in electrical engineering.

Research Interests

  • Wireless Communications
